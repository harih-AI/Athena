Cloud Computing Summary

Video Title:

Cloud Computing In 6 Minutes | What Is Cloud Computing? | Cloud Computing Explained | Simplilearn

Summary:

Cloud computing is a transformative technology that enables users to access and manage data and applications over the internet instead of relying on local servers or personal computers. This video explains the key concepts of cloud computing, including its benefits such as flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency. Viewers will learn about different cloud service models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and how they can leverage cloud solutions to enhance business operations and productivity. The concise explanation provided in just six minutes makes it an excellent introduction for beginners.
